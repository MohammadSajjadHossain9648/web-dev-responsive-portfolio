FAQs

Find answers to your most frequently asked Fitness questions.

Q. What are the gymâ€™s operating hours?

A: Our gym is open 24/7 to accommodate all schedules. Staffed hours are from 6 AM to 10 PM.

Q: Do I need to book classes in advance?

A: Yes, we recommend booking classes in advance to secure your spot as they can fill up quickly.

Q: What types of membership plans are available?

A: We offer basic, premium and family membership plans. Each plan includes access to all gym facilities, classes, and a complimentary fitness assessment.

Q: Are personal training sessions available?

A: Yes, we have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. You can book sessions directly through our website or at the front desk.

Q: What should I bring to the gym?

A: Bring a water bottle, towel, and appropriate workout attire. Lockers are available for personal belongings.

Q: Can I freeze my membership?

A: Yes, we allow members to freeze their membership up to 60 days at a time, with a maximum of 120 days in a calendar year. If you would like to freeze your membership, please call the front desk for assistance.

Q: Is there a cancellation fee for memberships?

A: We offer flexible cancellation policies with no fee for basic memberships. Other memberships may incur a small cancellation fee.

Q: How can I access online workout sessions?

A: Members can access our virtual training sessions through our mobile app or website. Simply log in with your membership details.

Q: What forms of payment does Fitness accept for automatic payments?

Fitness accepts credit cards, debit cards, and bank account transfers for automatic payments.
